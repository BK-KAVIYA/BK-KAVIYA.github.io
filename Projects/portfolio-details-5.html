Hostel Management System

Project information

  • Technology: Springboot, XML, Java, Hibernate, Retrofit Library, MySQL
  • Project Role: Team Leader
  • Project date: 10 Octomber, 2023
  • Project URL: GitHub

The Faculty of Technology at the University of Ruhuna, Sri Lanka, recognized the need to transition from outdated, manual processes to cutting-edge, automated solution. Our system is mainly designed to manage efficiently the hostel assets, and the process of recovering damaged assets. Apart from that main purpose, the system will assist hostel administrators in saving student records, payables, student attendance, student notices, and information about their rooms, as well as avoiding manual work. This report is a document of the rigorous development, deployment, and development processes undertaken to introduce the Hostel Assets Management System, which redefines various aspects of hostel administration.
