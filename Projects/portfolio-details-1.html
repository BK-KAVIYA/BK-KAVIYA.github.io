Revolutionizing Car Rentals: A Mobile App for Southern Lanka Car Rent & Tour

Project information

  • Technology: Java, XML ,MySQL
  • Project Role: Team Leader
  • Project date: 10 July, 2023
  • Project URL: GitHub

As the team leader for our third-year group project module, our team embarked on the development of a mobile app for "Southern Lanka Car Rent & Tour." This initiative arose from the company's recognition of the inefficiencies and errors within their manual system, particularly as they expand their fleet. My role as team leader involved coordinating the team's activities, setting project objectives, managing timelines, assigning tasks, fostering effective communication among team members, and serving as a liaison with external stakeholders, including the client. Through this project, we aimed to create a user-friendly mobile application that empowers customers to easily browse and book their preferred vehicles, ultimately enhancing the car rental services offered by "Southern Lanka Car Rent & Tour."
